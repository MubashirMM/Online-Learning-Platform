FAQs

What is EduConnect?

EduConnect is an online learning platform providing various courses and tutorials.

How do I sign up?

You can sign up by clicking on the Register button and filling in your details.

Are the courses free?

Some courses are free, while others require a subscription.

Can I get a certificate?

Yes, a certificate is provided upon course completion.

How do I reset my password?

You can reset your password by clicking on 'Forgot Password' on the login page.

Is there a mobile app?

Currently, we are developing a mobile version for better accessibility.

How can I contact support?

You can contact support through our Help Center or email us at support@educonnect.com.

Can I download course materials?

Yes, some courses allow you to download resources for offline use.

Do you offer refunds?

Refunds are available within the first 7 days of purchase.

Can I teach on EduConnect?

Yes, you can apply to be an instructor through our Instructor Portal.
